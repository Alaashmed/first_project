History of Our Bakery

Dream Bakery began on April 26th, 2015 in the city of Gaza. Focusing on the goals of “Quality, Healthy, and Tradition” the company is family operation making each dish in our own kitchens from scratch.Dream Bakery has three generations of family member working together to fulfill the idea “Honor the Customer, Quality Comes First”. Through hard work and dedication, Dream Bakery began building its brand around the idea of delicious and authentic traditional baked goods.
